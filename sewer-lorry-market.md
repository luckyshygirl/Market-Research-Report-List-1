<p><p>A sewer lorry, also known as a vacuum truck or a vacuum lorry, is a specialized vehicle used for clearing and transporting waste materials from sewers, septic tanks, and other similar storage systems. Sewer lorries are equipped with powerful vacuum systems that can suck up various types of waste materials and store them in a tank for disposal or treatment.</p><p>The global sewer lorry market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. The increasing urbanization and population growth in many developing countries have led to the demand for improved sanitation infrastructure, driving the need for sewer cleaning and maintenance services.
