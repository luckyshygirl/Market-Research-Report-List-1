<p><p>The Dura Substitution Prostheses market includes two types: Biological Dura Substitution Prostheses and Synthetic Dura Substitution Prostheses. Biological Dura Substitution Prostheses are made from natural tissues, such as human or animal dura mater, and are used to replace the damaged dura mater in the brain or spinal cord. On the other hand, Synthetic Dura Substitution Prostheses are artificial materials, like silicone, that are designed to mimic the properties of the dura mater and are used as a replacement for the damaged or defective dura mater.</p></p>
