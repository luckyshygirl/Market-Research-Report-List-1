<p><p>Square finned tubes are a type of heat exchanger component used in various industries. There are two main market types for square finned tubes: single metal finned tubes and bimetal composite finned tubes. Single metal finned tubes are made of a single material, such as copper or aluminum, and are cost-effective but have limited corrosion resistance. On the other hand, bimetal composite finned tubes are created by bonding different metals together, offering enhanced corrosion resistance and thermal efficiency, but they are more expensive. Both types serve the purpose of improving heat transfer in applications requiring efficient cooling or heating.</p></p>
