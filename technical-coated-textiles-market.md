<p><p>Technical coated textiles refer to fabrics that are coated or treated with various materials to enhance their performance and functionality. PU-coated textiles involve the application of polyurethane to the fabric for improved water resistance and durability. Rubber-coated textiles use rubber compounds to achieve properties like elasticity and resistance to extreme temperatures. Silicone-coated textiles employ silicone compounds to provide heat and chemical resistance. PVC-coated textiles are fabric coated with polyvinyl chloride for waterproofing and increased strength. Other coated textiles may include materials like fluorocarbon and acrylic for specific purposes, such as UV resistance or fire retardancy.</p></p>
