<p><p>Mineral Wool Board Insulation market consists of three main types: Slag Wool, Glass Wool, and Rock Wool. Slag Wool is produced through the melting and spinning of blast furnace slag. It is known for its high temperature resistance, making it suitable for insulation in industrial applications. Glass Wool is made from recycled glass and boasts excellent thermal and acoustic insulation properties. Rock Wool is manufactured from natural rock materials like basalt and diabase, offering effective fire resistance and sound absorption. These types of mineral wool provide reliable insulation solutions for various sectors.</p></p>
