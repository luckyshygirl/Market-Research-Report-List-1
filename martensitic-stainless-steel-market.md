<p><p>Martensitic stainless steel is categorized into various types based on their compositions and specific properties. Type 403 is a basic chromium stainless steel with good corrosion resistance and limited heat resistance. Type 410 is a general-purpose stainless steel known for its excellent hardness, high strength, and corrosion resistance. Type 414 offers improved wear resistance and higher mechanical properties compared to Type 410. Type 416 contains sulfur to enhance machinability and is highly suitable for parts requiring considerable machining. "Others" represents additional martensitic stainless steel alloys with distinct characteristics and applications.</p></p>
