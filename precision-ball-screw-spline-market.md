<p><p>Precision ball screw spline is a mechanical device used in various industries for linear motion applications. It consists of a ball screw and a spline nut, which work together to convert rotary motion into linear motion. The ball screw contains a threaded shaft with grooves that correspond to the balls located inside the spline nut. As the screw rotates, the balls move along the grooves, resulting in linear movement of the nut.</p><p>The precision ball screw spline market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years.
