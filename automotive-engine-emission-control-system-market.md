<p><p>The automotive engine emission control system market includes various components such as oxygen sensors, EGR valves, catalytic converters, air pumps, PCV valves, and charcoal canisters. Oxygen sensors monitor the oxygen level in the exhaust gases, while EGR valves recirculate some of the exhaust gases back into the engine to lower emissions. Catalytic converters convert harmful gases into less harmful ones. Air pumps inject fresh air into the exhaust system for better combustion. PCV valves regulate the ventilation of the engine crankcase. Charcoal canisters trap and store fuel vapors from the fuel tank to prevent them from being released into the atmosphere.</p></p>
