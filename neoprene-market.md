<p><p>The Neoprene market consists of various types, including Normal Linear Grades, Precrosslinked Grades, Sulfur Modified Grades, and Slow Crystallizing Grades. Normal Linear Grades are standard neoprene materials offering good tensile strength and flexibility. Precrosslinked Grades are pre-vulcanized for easy processing and improved physical properties. Sulfur Modified Grades provide enhanced chemical resistance and heat aging properties. Slow Crystallizing Grades are designed to crystallize slowly, providing longer processing times and better dimensional stability. Each type caters to different industrial needs, offering specific characteristics to suit various applications.</p></p>
