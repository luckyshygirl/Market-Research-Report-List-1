As automotive production continues to rise globally, the demand for connecting rod bearings will also increase.</p><p>Moreover, the growing emphasis on fuel efficiency and reduced emissions is driving the development of lightweight and high-performance connecting rod bearings. Manufacturers are investing in research and development activities to develop advanced materials and designs that can withstand high temperatures and pressures, thus improving engine efficiency.</p><p>Additionally, the rise of electric vehicles (EVs) is expected to impact the market positively. Although EVs have fewer components compared to traditional internal combustion engines, they still require connecting rod bearings for the smooth operation of any auxiliary combustion engines or range extenders, making the market resilient.</p><p>The current outlook of the vehicle connecting rod bearing market is also positive.
