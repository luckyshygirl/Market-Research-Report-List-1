<p><p>Cardiac care equipment market includes various types of equipment to monitor and treat heart-related conditions. Cardiac monitors are used to continuously monitor the heart's electrical activity. EKG equipment is used to record the heart's electrical signals in the form of an electrocardiogram. Heart-lung bypass machines are utilized during open-heart surgeries to temporarily take over the heart's function. Defibrillators are used to deliver an electric shock to restore normal heart rhythm. Intra-aortic balloon pumps assist the heart in pumping blood during specific cardiac conditions. Other equipment includes pacemakers, ventricular assist devices, and cardiac catheters.</p></p>
