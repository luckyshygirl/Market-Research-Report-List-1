<p><p>The adhesive coatings market encompasses various types of coatings used for adhesion purposes. Non-reactive based adhesives are commonly used due to their high bond strength and resistance to temperature. Solvent-based adhesives, on the other hand, contain solvents that evaporate during application, leaving a solid adhesive behind. Water-based dispersion adhesives utilize water as a carrier for adhesive particles, providing good environmental compatibility. Lastly, other adhesive types include hot-melt adhesives, pressure-sensitive adhesives, and UV-curable adhesives, each offering unique properties suitable for specific applications.</p></p>
