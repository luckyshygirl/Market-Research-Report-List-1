<p><p>The parallel sorting robot market encompasses two main types: two-axis robots and multi-axis robots. Two-axis robots are designed to move objects in two dimensions, typically back-and-forth or up-and-down. They are commonly used for simple sorting tasks and have a simpler structure. On the other hand, multi-axis robots offer more flexibility as they can move objects in multiple directions, enabling complex sorting operations. They are widely used in industries requiring intricate sorting processes, such as electronics manufacturing or logistics, and incorporate advanced technologies for precise and efficient sorting.</p></p>
