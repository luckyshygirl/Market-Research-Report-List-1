The increasing demand for electric vehicles (EVs) and the rising adoption of portable electronic devices are the primary drivers for this market. <p><p>The lithium battery diaphragm market offers three types of separators for lithium-ion batteries. Firstly, the Monolayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator, which consists of a single layer that acts as a barrier between the positive and negative electrodes. Secondly, the Bilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator, which comprises two layers for improved performance and enhanced safety. Lastly, the Trilayer Lithium-Ion Battery Separator includes three layers that offer even better thermal stability and higher mechanical strength. These separators play a crucial role in preventing short circuits within the battery and regulating ion flow for optimal performance.</p></p>
