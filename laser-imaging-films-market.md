<p><p>Laser imaging films refer to a type of film used in the printing and imaging industry. Dry-type film and wet-type film are two common market types in this sector. Dry-type film is a self-developing film that does not require any additional processing chemicals. It is convenient and time-saving. On the other hand, wet-type film needs to be processed with specific chemical solutions for image development. While wet-type film offers higher image quality and resolution, it requires more steps and materials for processing.</p></p>
