<p><p>The Double-Clad Fiber Laser market can be categorized into two types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. Continuous Wave Fiber Lasers provide a continuous laser emission, which is suitable for applications that require a steady and constant laser beam. Pulsed Fiber Lasers, on the other hand, emit laser pulses with high peak powers and short durations, making them suitable for applications that require high-energy output in brief bursts. Both types serve different purposes and cater to various industries based on their specific requirements.</p></p>
