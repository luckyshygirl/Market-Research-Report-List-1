<p><p>The commercial dish dispenser market encompasses two main types: heated style and unheated style. Heated style dish dispensers are equipped with heating elements to keep the dishes warm and ready for use. They are commonly used in settings where hot dishes are served, such as hotels or buffets. On the other hand, unheated style dish dispensers do not have any heating mechanism and are typically used for storing and dispensing room temperature dishes, commonly found in cafeterias or quick-service restaurants. Both types cater to different needs and preferences within the foodservice industry.</p></p>
