<p><p>The Carpets and Rugs Market is diverse, comprising various types. Woven carpets are constructed by weaving yarns together, resulting in a durable and aesthetically pleasing product. Needle felt carpets are created by interlocking fibers through a mechanical process, providing excellent sound absorption. Knotted carpets are made by hand-tying knots onto a woven foundation, offering intricate designs and high-quality craftsmanship. Other types include tufted carpets, which use a backing fabric and tufting machine, and flatweave carpets, which are woven on a loom without any pile. The market offers a wide range of choices to cater to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
