<p><p>The Needle-Free Influenza Vaccine Market consists of two types: Live Attenuated Influenza Vaccine (LAIV) and Split Influenza Vaccine. LAIV is a type of vaccine that contains live, weakened influenza viruses and is administered through nasal spray. It stimulates an immune response without causing illness. On the other hand, Split Influenza Vaccine is created by splitting the influenza virus into its components, namely the outer envelope and inner protein. This type of vaccine is usually injected and helps the body recognize and fight against influenza viruses.</p></p>
