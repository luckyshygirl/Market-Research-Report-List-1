<p><p>Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene (ABS) panel market is segmented into three types: Emulsion Graft Copolymerization, Bulk Copolymerization, and Other markets. Emulsion Graft Copolymerization involves the emulsion polymerization process where an ABS copolymer is formed by grafting styrene-acrylonitrile (SAN) onto a butadiene rubber substrate. Bulk Copolymerization refers to the production of ABS by copolymerizing acrylonitrile, butadiene, and styrene in bulk. The Other market segment includes additional methods for ABS panel production, such as suspension graft copolymerization or hybrid technologies. These different types cater to specific manufacturing requirements and offer distinct properties in terms of mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and thermal stability.</p></p>
