<p><p>The adhesive accelerator market can be classified into two types: one-component and two-component. In the one-component market, the accelerators are pre-mixed with the adhesive, creating a ready-to-use product. This type simplifies the application process as no additional mixing is required. On the other hand, the two-component market involves separate containers for the adhesive and accelerator. They are mixed together just before application, offering greater control over the curing time and the ability to customize the adhesive properties. Both markets cater to different adhesive application needs and offer distinct advantages in terms of convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
