<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Walk-in coolers and freezers are essential equipment for storing perishable goods in various industries. There are three market types commonly available: self-contained, remote condensing, and multiplex condensing. Self-contained units consist of both the refrigeration system and the storage space, making them easier to install but limited in size. Remote condensing units have the refrigeration system placed outside the storage area, providing larger storage capacity and reducing noise levels. Multiplex condensing units combine multiple storage spaces with a shared refrigeration system, offering greater flexibility and energy efficiency. These market types cater to different needs and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
