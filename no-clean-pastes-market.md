<p><p>The no-clean pastes market can be divided into two main types: lead-containing and lead-free. Lead-containing pastes traditionally used lead-based solder materials in their compositions. However, due to environmental concerns and regulations, many manufacturers have shifted towards lead-free pastes which utilize alternative materials like tin-silver-copper alloys. Both types of pastes are used in electronic assembly processes for soldering components onto circuit boards. While lead-containing pastes are being phased out, lead-free pastes are becoming the preferred choice due to their safer and more sustainable nature.</p></p>
