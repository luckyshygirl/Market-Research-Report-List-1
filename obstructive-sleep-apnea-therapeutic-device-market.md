<p><p>The Obstructive Sleep Apnea Therapeutic Device market includes two main types of devices: PSG (Polysomnography) and Pulse Oximeter. PSG devices monitor multiple signals during sleep, such as brain waves, breathing, and heart rate, to diagnose sleep disorders. This device helps healthcare professionals analyze data and determine the appropriate treatment for sleep apnea patients. On the other hand, Pulse Oximeters are portable devices that measure oxygen saturation levels in the blood and heart rate, providing continuous monitoring for individuals with sleep apnea or other respiratory conditions.</p></p>
