<p><p>The market for Aircraft Engine Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC) can be categorized into various types based on the composition of the composites. Oxide, Silicon Carbide, Carbon, and Others are the main types. Oxide composites consist of ceramics like alumina, while Silicon Carbide composites contain silicon carbide fibers. Carbon composites are composed of carbon fibers embedded in a carbon matrix. The "Others" category includes composites made of non-oxide ceramics like silicon nitride. These different types of CMCs offer varying properties and benefits, which contribute to their specific applications in the aircraft engine industry.</p></p>
