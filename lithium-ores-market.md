<p><p>The lithium ore market is composed of two main types of lithium sources: saline lake lithium and mineral lithium. Saline lake lithium refers to lithium salts that are extracted from underground brine pools found in salt lakes. These brines are typically rich in lithium minerals due to the high evaporation rate and natural concentration process. On the other hand, mineral lithium involves the extraction of lithium from hard rock deposits through mining and processing techniques. Both sources contribute to the global lithium supply and cater to different market demands.</p></p>
