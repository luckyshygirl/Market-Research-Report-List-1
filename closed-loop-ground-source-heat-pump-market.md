It consists of a closed-loop of underground pipes filled with a heat transfer fluid that circulates between the heat pump and the ground, absorbing heat in the winter and releasing heat in the summer.</p><p>The future outlook of the Closed-Loop GSHP market is highly promising. <p><p>Closed-Loop Ground Source Heat Pump (GSHP) market is divided into three main types: Horizontal GSHP, Vertical GSHP, and Surface Water GSHP. </p><p>Horizontal GSHP utilizes a network of underground pipes installed horizontally in the ground. Vertical GSHP, on the other hand, uses deep boreholes drilled vertically into the ground. Lastly, Surface Water GSHP extracts heat from nearby bodies of water, such as rivers, lakes, or ponds. These various market types address different geographical and environmental conditions, providing efficient and eco-friendly heating and cooling solutions for residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
