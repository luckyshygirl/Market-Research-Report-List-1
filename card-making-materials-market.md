ABS, or acrylonitrile butadiene styrene, is known for its rigidity and impact resistance. PHA, or polyhydroxyalkanoates, is a biodegradable and renewable material. PC, or polycarbonate, is a strong material known for its resistance to heat and impact. These materials are utilized in the card-making industry to create high-quality, customizable, and long-lasting cards.</p></p>
