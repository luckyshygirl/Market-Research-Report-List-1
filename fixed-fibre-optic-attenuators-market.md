<p><p>Fixed fibre optic attenuators are used to reduce the power level of optical signals in fibre optic networks. They come in different types, including bulkhead, plug, panel mount, and in-line jumper attenuators. </p><p>Bulkhead attenuators are designed to be mounted directly onto the bulkhead of optical patch panels or other mounting devices. Plug attenuators resemble the shape of a typical fibre optic connector and can be easily inserted into optical ports. Panel mount attenuators are fixed onto an adapter panel or a holding shelf within a patch panel. In-line jumper attenuators are integrated directly into fibre optic patch cables.</p><p>Overall, these attenuators serve the same purpose of adjusting signal power but are configured differently based on the installation requirements.</p></p>
