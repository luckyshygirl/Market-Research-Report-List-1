<p><p>Fixed Line Broadband Access Equipment can be categorized into three types: </p><p>1. IP-based: This type of equipment uses Internet Protocol (IP) to transmit data over the broadband network. IP-based equipment is widely used due to its compatibility with various applications and protocols, enabling seamless integration with different devices.</p><p>2. ATM cell-based: Equipment based on Asynchronous Transfer Mode (ATM) cells utilizes a fixed cell size for data transmission, ensuring a consistent and reliable delivery of data. ATM-based equipment is known for its low latency and high quality of service.</p><p>3. TDMA-based: Time Division Multiple Access (TDMA) is a technology used for dividing the data transmission into time slots, allowing multiple users to share the same frequency channel. TDMA-based equipment efficiently utilizes available bandwidth, providing reliable connectivity to multiple users simultaneously.</p></p>
