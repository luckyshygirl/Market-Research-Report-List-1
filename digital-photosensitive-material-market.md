<p><p>The Digital Photosensitive Material Market includes two main types of chips: CCD (Charge-Coupled Device) and CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor). A CCD chip is known for superior image quality, low noise, and high sensitivity, making it suitable for professional photography and scientific applications. On the other hand, CMOS chips offer lower power consumption, faster data readouts, and a more cost-effective solution, making them widely used in consumer electronics like smartphones and digital cameras. Both chips play a crucial role in capturing and converting light into digital information, but they differ in performance and application areas.</p></p>
