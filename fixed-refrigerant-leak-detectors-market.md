<p><p>The fixed refrigerant leak detectors market comprises two types: Halide and Electronic. Halide leak detectors utilize a chemical reaction between the halide-based solution and the refrigerant gas to detect and locate leaks. They are cost-effective and suitable for small-scale setups. On the other hand, Electronic leak detectors use advanced sensor technology to monitor refrigerant levels continuously, providing accurate and instantaneous leakage detection. They are widely adopted in large-scale commercial and industrial applications due to their high sensitivity and reliability.</p></p>
