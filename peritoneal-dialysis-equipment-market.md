<p><p>The Peritoneal Dialysis Equipment Market includes two types: Continuous Ambulatory Peritoneal Dialysis (CAPD) and Automated Peritoneal Dialysis (APD). CAPD involves manual exchanges of dialysis fluid performed by the patient throughout the day, allowing for flexibility and freedom. In contrast, APD utilizes a machine called a cycler to automatically perform several dialysis exchanges during the night while the patient is asleep, providing convenience and ease. Both types offer effective alternatives to traditional hemodialysis for patients with kidney failure, allowing them to receive regular, at-home treatments.</p></p>
