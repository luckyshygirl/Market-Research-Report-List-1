They are widely used in various applications where flexibility is desired, such as adhesives, coatings, composites, and electrical laminates. Rubber modified flexible epoxy resins are formulated by incorporating rubber particles into the epoxy matrix, enhancing their flexibility. Urethane modified flexible epoxy resins are made by blending urethane oligomers with epoxy resins, resulting in improved flexibility and elongation properties. Dimer acid is a fatty acid used in the production of flexible epoxy resins, providing enhanced flexibility and toughness to the final product.</p></p>
