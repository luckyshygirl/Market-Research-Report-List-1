<p><strong>The Oil-Well Cement Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ordinary</li><li>Moderate Sulfate-Resistant</li><li>High Sulfate-Resistant</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Oil-well cement is categorized into three types based on their sulfate resistance capabilities. Ordinary oil-well cement is suitable for wells with low sulfate concentration. Moderate sulfate-resistant cement is designed for wells with moderate sulfate levels. It exhibits better resistance to sulfates, preventing deterioration. High sulfate-resistant cement is specifically manufactured for wells with high sulfate levels. Its enhanced sulfate resistance ensures long-term durability and integrity of wellbore casing, even in corrosive environments. These different types of cement help ensure the integrity and longevity of oil wells under varying sulfate conditions.</p></p>
