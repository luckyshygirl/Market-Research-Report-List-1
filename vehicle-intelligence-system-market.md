<p><p>The Vehicle Intelligence System market encompasses three main types: Real-Time Traffic System (RTS), Road Sign Detection System (RSD), and Navigation and Vehicle Surveillance System (NVS). RTS is designed to provide drivers with real-time information on road conditions, traffic congestion, and accidents. RSD identifies and interprets road signs through cameras or sensors, alerting drivers of important traffic information. NVS combines global positioning systems (GPS) and cameras to provide drivers with accurate navigation guidance and enhance vehicle surveillance for security purposes. These systems are integral in enhancing driver safety, vehicle efficiency, and overall road experience.</p></p>
