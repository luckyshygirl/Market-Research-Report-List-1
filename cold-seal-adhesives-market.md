Water-based adhesives are environmentally friendly and offer good adhesion on various substrates. Solvent-based adhesives provide high bond strength and fast curing time, but contain volatile organic compounds. Solvent-less adhesives are free from solvents and offer excellent adhesion and flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different applications based on specific requirements.</p></p>
