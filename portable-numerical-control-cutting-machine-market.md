<p><p>The portable numerical control cutting machine market is divided into two types: flame cutting and plasma cutting. Flame cutting involves the use of fuel gases and oxygen to create a high-temperature flame that melts and cuts metal materials. On the other hand, plasma cutting utilizes a high-velocity jet of ionized gas to heat and melt the metal, which is then blown away by the gas stream. Both flame cutting and plasma cutting are popular methods in the industry and offer precise and efficient cutting capabilities for various applications.</p></p>
