Among the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand for efficient and error-free pharmaceutical manufacturing processes. One such trend is the integration of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML) technologies into automation systems. RPA can significantly reduce errors, enhance speed and accuracy, and improve compliance in tasks such as data entry, inventory management, and regulatory reporting.</p><p>In conclusion, the pharmaceutical industry automation market is expected to grow at a significant rate in the coming years.
