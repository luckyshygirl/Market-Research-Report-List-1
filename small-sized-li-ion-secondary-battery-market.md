<p><p>The small-sized Li-ion secondary battery market consists of three main types: cylindrical cell, prismatic cell, and polymer cell. Cylindrical cells are most commonly used and have a cylindrical shape. Prismatic cells have a rectangular shape and are often used in slim devices. Polymer cells are made with a flexible polymer material, allowing for customization in shape and size. Each type has its advantages, such as high energy density in cylindrical cells, compactness in prismatic cells, and flexibility in polymer cells, catering to different device requirements.</p></p>
