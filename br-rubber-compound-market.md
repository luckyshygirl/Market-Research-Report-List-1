<p><p>The BR rubber compound market includes two types: CIIR and BIIR. CIIR stands for Chlorobutyl Isobutene Isoprene Rubber, while BIIR stands for Bromobutyl Isobutene Isoprene Rubber. CIIR rubber compound offers excellent ozone and weather resistance, as well as good chemical resistance. It is commonly used in the production of tire inner liners, pharmaceutical closures, and automotive parts. On the other hand, BIIR rubber compound provides superior heat resistance, good aging characteristics, and low permeability to gases and moisture. It is widely utilized in applications such as tire curing bladders, fuel hoses, and pharmaceutical stoppers.</p></p>
