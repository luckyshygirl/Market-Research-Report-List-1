<p><p>The metal coated glass microspheres market is categorized into two types: hollow and solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are tiny glass particles with empty centers, providing them with a lighter weight and improved buoyancy. These microspheres are commonly used in applications such as cosmetics and paints, where their low density is advantageous. On the other hand, solid microspheres have no empty centers and are heavier than hollow ones. They find applications in industries like aerospace, automotive, and electronics due to their high strength, thermal resistance, and durability.</p></p>
