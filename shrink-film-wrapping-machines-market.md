<p><p>Shrink film wrapping machines are classified into two types: fully-automatic and semi-automatic. Fully-automatic machines are designed to perform the entire wrapping process automatically, including product loading, film sealing, and heat shrinking. These machines are ideal for large-scale production and require minimal human intervention. On the other hand, semi-automatic machines require some manual assistance during the wrapping process, such as product loading and film sealing. These machines are suitable for small to medium-scale production and offer a more cost-effective option compared to fully-automatic machines.</p></p>
