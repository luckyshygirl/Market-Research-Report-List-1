ORP sensors measure the oxidizing or reducing potential of a solution, which indicates its ability to oxidize or reduce substances. Both sensors are crucial in maintaining the desired quality and safety of a solution.</p><p>The pH & ORP Sensors Market is witnessing significant growth due to various factors. They offer features like easy calibration, temperature compensation, and integration capabilities with other devices or systems. Both types cater to different customer needs and applications in various industries.</p></p>
