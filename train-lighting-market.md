<p><p>The train lighting market encompasses various types of lighting technologies, including fluorescent, halogen, LED, and xenon. Fluorescent lighting relies on the excitation of gases to produce light and is known for its energy efficiency. Halogen lighting uses a tungsten filament and halogen gas to produce bright and long-lasting light. LED (Light Emitting Diode) lighting is highly efficient, durable, and offers various color options. Xenon lighting utilizes xenon gas to produce a bright, bluish-white light. These different lighting options cater to the specific needs and preferences of the train industry.</p></p>
