<p><p>The marine refrigerator-freezer market encompasses two main types: top-loading and front-loading models. Top-loading refrigerators have a lid on top that opens vertically, allowing users to access the contents easily. They are generally more compact and suitable for smaller spaces. In contrast, front-loading refrigerators feature a door on the front that swings open horizontally, similar to household refrigerators. This type offers larger capacity and easier organization of items. Both types serve the marine industry, helping to store and preserve food and beverages in boats and ships.</p></p>
