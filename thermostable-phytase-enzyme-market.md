<p><p>The thermostable phytase enzyme market includes different types of enzymes: Histidine acid phosphatases (HAPs), Β-propeller phytases, Purple acid phosphatases, and Protein tyrosine phosphatase-like phytases. HAPs are enzymes that play a vital role in phosphorus releasing from phytate and are widely used in animal feed. Β-propeller phytases are another type that improves phosphorus availability in feed. Purple acid phosphatases help in the breakdown of phytic acid to release nutrients, and Protein tyrosine phosphatase-like phytases are involved in dephosphorylating phytic acid. These enzymes contribute to enhancing the nutritional value of animal feed.</p></p>
