<p><p>The Automotive Window Sealing Systems market includes three major types of materials - EPDM, TPE, and PVC - used for sealing automotive windows. EPDM (Ethylene Propylene Diene Monomer) is a synthetic rubber known for its durability, weather resistance, and ease of installation, making it a popular choice. TPE (Thermoplastic Elastomer) is a versatile material that offers a combination of elastomeric properties and easy processing. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is a widely used thermoplastic known for its affordability, flexibility, and resistance to water and chemicals. These materials cater to various automotive sealing needs, offering different benefits and applications in the market.</p></p>
