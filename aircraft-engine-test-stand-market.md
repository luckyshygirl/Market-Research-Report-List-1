<p><ul><li>Loaded Thermal Test</li><li>No-load Thermal Test</li><li>Non-ignition Test</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Engine Test Stand market has three main types. The Loaded Thermal Test involves testing engines while they are running at full power and simulating real operating conditions. The No-load Thermal Test is similar, but with engines running without power applied to the output shaft. The Non-ignition Test involves testing engines without igniting the fuel, allowing for the evaluation of components like seals and bearings. These different types cater to the specific needs of aircraft engine manufacturers and help ensure safe and reliable engine performance.</p></p>
