RDT provides quick results by detecting the presence of antibodies in blood samples. ELISA is a more sophisticated method that uses enzymes to detect antibodies and is commonly used in laboratories. Neutralization Assay measures the ability of antibodies to neutralize the virus, providing valuable information on the effectiveness of the immune response. These different types cater to various needs and applications in COVID-19 testing.</p></p>
