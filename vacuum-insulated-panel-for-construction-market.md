<p><p>The Vacuum Insulated Panel (VIP) for construction market includes two major types: Fumed Silica and Glass Fiber. Fumed Silica is a type of insulation material made from silicon dioxide particles. It has high thermal resistance properties and is widely used in VIPs to reduce heat transfer. Glass Fiber, on the other hand, is a common insulation material made from fine fibers of glass. It offers excellent thermal insulation and is commonly used in VIPs for its lightweight and durable characteristics. Both Fumed Silica and Glass Fiber play a crucial role in enhancing energy efficiency and reducing heat loss in construction projects.</p></p>
