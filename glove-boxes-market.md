<p><p>Glove boxes are specialized containment systems used in various industries where the manipulation of substances in a controlled environment is required. There are two main types of glove boxes: stainless steel and plastic. Stainless steel glove boxes offer durability, high resistance to chemicals, and greater protection against contamination. They are commonly used in research laboratories and industries that require stringent cleanliness standards. On the other hand, plastic glove boxes are cost-effective and suitable for less demanding applications. They are commonly used in educational settings and small-scale laboratories.</p></p>
