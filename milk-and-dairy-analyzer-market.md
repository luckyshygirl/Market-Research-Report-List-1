<p><p>The milk and dairy analyzer market consists of two primary types: ultrasonic and infrared. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize sound waves to measure various parameters in milk and dairy products, such as fat content, protein content, and milk density. They are known for their accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, infrared analyzers utilize infrared radiation to determine the chemical composition of milk and dairy products. They provide quick and non-destructive analysis, making them suitable for mass production and quality control applications in the dairy industry.</p></p>
