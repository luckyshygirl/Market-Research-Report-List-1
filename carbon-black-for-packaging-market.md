<p><p>Carbon Black is used in the packaging industry in two different forms: Thermal Carbon Black Products and Furnace Carbon Black Products. Thermal Carbon Black Products are derived from catalytic pyrolysis processes and have high surface area and porosity, making them ideal for applications requiring high purity and conductivity. On the other hand, Furnace Carbon Black Products are produced through the controlled combustion of hydrocarbons and are known for their reinforcing properties, making them suitable for applications where strength and durability are important.</p></p>
