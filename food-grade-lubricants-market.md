<p><p>The food grade lubricants market is categorized into three types: mineral oil, synthetic oil, and bio-based oil. Mineral oil is derived from petroleum and is widely used due to its cost-effectiveness and high thermal stability. Synthetic oil, on the other hand, is artificially manufactured using chemicals, providing enhanced lubrication properties and resistance to temperature extremes. Bio-based oil is derived from renewable sources like plants and offers eco-friendly alternatives to traditional lubricants. All three types have their unique properties and applications in the food industry.</p></p>
