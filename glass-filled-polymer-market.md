<p><p>Glass-filled polymer is a type of composite material that is made by combining a thermoplastic resin with glass fibers. The market is driven by the increasing demand from various end-use industries such as automotive, aerospace, electrical and electronics, consumer goods, and construction. Glass-filled polymers are widely used in automotive applications due to their lightweight properties and high strength, which helps in reducing the overall weight of vehicles and improving fuel efficiency. Moreover, the growing construction industry is also driving the demand for glass-filled polymers as they are used in applications such as windows, doors, roofing, and flooring.</p><p>One of the key trends in the glass-filled polymer market is the increasing focus on sustainable materials. Polyester glass-filled polymers exhibit good mechanical properties and heat resistance, commonly used in consumer goods and electrical components.
