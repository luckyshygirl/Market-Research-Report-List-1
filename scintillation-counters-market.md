<p><p>Scintillation counters are types of radiation detection devices used to measure and detect ionizing radiation. They comprise a scintillator material that emits light photons when it interacts with radiation, and a photomultiplier tube (PMT) or other light detector to convert the emitted light into an electrical signal. These detectors are widely used in various industries, including healthcare, nuclear power plants, environmental monitoring, and research laboratories.</p><p>The current outlook of the scintillation counters market is positive, driven by increasing applications in the healthcare sector, nuclear power plants, and research institutes.
