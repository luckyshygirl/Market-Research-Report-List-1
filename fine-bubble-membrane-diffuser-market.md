<p><p>The Fine Bubble Membrane Diffuser market consists of two main types: Disc Diffuser and Tube Diffuser. Disc Diffusers are circular-shaped diffusers that release small bubble sizes, resulting in high oxygen transfer efficiency. They are commonly used for wastewater treatment and aeration systems in industries. On the other hand, Tube Diffusers are cylindrical-shaped diffusers that release fine bubbles through tiny holes. They are typically installed in linear arrays and provide uniform distribution of oxygen. Tube Diffusers are commonly found in municipal wastewater treatment plants, aquaculture systems, and industrial applications.</p></p>
