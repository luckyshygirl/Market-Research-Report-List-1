<p><p>The IoT wireless sensor market can be classified into two main types: traditional wireless technology and LPWANs technology. On the other hand, LPWANs (Low-Power Wide Area Networks) technology is designed for long-range communication with low power consumption. It enables IoT devices to transmit data over longer distances with minimal battery usage, making it ideal for applications that require wide area coverage and extended battery life.</p></p>
