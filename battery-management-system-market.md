<p><p>A Battery Management System (BMS) is an electronic system that manages and monitors the charging and discharging of batteries. The market is being driven by factors such as the increasing adoption of electric vehicles (EVs) and hybrid electric vehicles (HEVs), growing demand for renewable energy storage systems, advancements in battery technologies, and the need for effective battery management solutions.</p><p>The market is also expected to be influenced by emerging trends such as the integration of BMS with cloud computing and the internet of things (IoT).
