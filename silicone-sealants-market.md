<p><strong>The Silicone Sealants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Silicone sealants are used for bonding, sealing, and insulation in numerous industries and applications. Pressure-sensitive sealants adhere to surfaces upon applying pressure, offering instant bonding. Radiation-curing sealants use ultraviolet or electron beam radiation to rapidly cure and form strong bonds. Room temperature vulcanizing sealants cure at ambient temperatures without the need for extra heat, suitable for a wide range of applications. Thermoset sealants harden irreversibly when exposed to heat, creating a durable and heat-resistant bond.</p></p>
