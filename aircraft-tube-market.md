They provide improved performance and durability compared to traditional metal tubes.</p><p>3. This technology allows for complex designs and reduced production time.</p><p>4. Focus on electric aircrafts: With the rising interest in electric propulsion for aircrafts, tube manufacturers are developing tubes specifically designed for electric power distribution and cooling systems.</p><p>5.
