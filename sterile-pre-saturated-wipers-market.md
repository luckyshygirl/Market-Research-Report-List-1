<p><p>The market for sterile pre-saturated wipers can be categorized into two types: isopropanol pre-saturated wipers and ethanol pre-saturated wipers. Both types of pre-saturated wipers are widely used in various industries for cleaning and maintaining a sterile environment.</p></p>
