<p><p>The Automotive Thyristor SCR market consists of two main types: Unidirectional Thyristor and Bidirectional Thyristor. </p><p>Unidirectional Thyristor is designed to conduct current in only one direction. It is commonly used in automotive applications where current flow needs to be controlled in a single direction, such as battery charging and motor control.</p><p>On the other hand, Bidirectional Thyristor is capable of conducting current in both directions. It is often used in automotive systems that require alternating current flow, such as inverter systems and power switches, allowing for efficient energy conversion and control.</p></p>
