<p><p>Automated Test Equipment (ATE) refers to computer-controlled systems used in the electronics industry to test and validate semiconductors and electronic components. The ATE market can be divided into two major types: Wafer ATE and Packaged Device ATE. Wafer ATE is employed during the production of semiconductor wafers and tests individual integrated circuits (ICs) before they are packaged. Conversely, Packaged Device ATE is used to test assembled ICs or electronic devices after they have been packaged. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the quality and reliability of electronic components before their deployment in various applications.</p></p>
