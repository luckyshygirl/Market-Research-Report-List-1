<p><p>The automotive vehicle-to-everything (V2X) market includes various types of communication between vehicles and different entities. Vehicle-to-vehicle (V2V) refers to direct communication between vehicles for improved safety and traffic efficiency. Vehicle-to-infrastructure (V2I) enables communication between vehicles and infrastructure, such as traffic lights, for enhanced traffic management. Vehicle-to-pedestrian (V2P) allows vehicles to exchange information with pedestrians, ensuring pedestrian safety. Vehicle-to-home (V2H) facilitates communication between vehicles and homes, enabling control of home appliances. Vehicle-to-grid (V2G) enables electric vehicles to contribute energy back to the grid. Vehicle-to-network (V2N) involves communication with cloud-based platforms, enabling real-time information sharing and remote management of vehicles.</p></p>
