<p><p>Baited insect traps market comprises various types of products, including bait stations, granular baits, and gel baits. Bait stations are small enclosed devices that hold attractant substances to lure insects and trap them. Granular baits are solid particles that are scattered on the ground or in specific areas to attract insects. Gel baits, on the other hand, are sticky substances placed in strategic locations to attract and entrap insects. All these products aim to effectively lure and control insects by providing them with an attractive source of food or shelter.</p></p>
