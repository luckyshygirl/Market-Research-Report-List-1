<p><p>An elastomeric coupling is a type of flexible coupling used to connect two rotating shafts in order to transmit torque and accommodate misalignments. It consists of an elastomeric element placed between two hubs, which absorb shock, dampen vibrations, and compensate for angular, parallel, and axial misalignments. Elastomeric couplings are widely used in various industries including automotive, mechanical engineering, oil and gas, and power generation.</p><p>The elastomeric coupling market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing demand for elastomeric couplings can be attributed to their advantages over other types of couplings, such as high torque transmission capacity, low maintenance, and easy installation.
