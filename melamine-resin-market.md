<p><p>Melamine Resin Market Types refer to the variations of melamine resins available in the market. Methylated Melamine Resin is a type where melamine is reacted with formaldehyde and methanol, increasing its stability and resistance to water, oil, and heat. This resin is commonly used in the production of laminates, coatings, and adhesives. On the other hand, Non-methylated Melamine Resin is produced without the addition of methanol. This type is primarily used in applications where color stability is crucial, such as in the manufacturing of decorative papers and inks.</p></p>
