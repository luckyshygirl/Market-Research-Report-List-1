<p><p>Enterprise Key Management (EKM) refers to the centralized management of cryptographic keys within an organization. There are two major market types for EKM: On Premise and Cloud. On Premise EKM involves deploying the key management solution within an organization's own infrastructure, providing complete control and security. Cloud EKM, on the other hand, involves outsourcing the key management to a cloud service provider, offering flexibility and convenience, but with potential concerns about security and data sovereignty. The choice between these two market types depends on an organization's specific security requirements and preferences.</p></p>
