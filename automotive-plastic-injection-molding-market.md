<p><p>The automotive plastic injection molding market is categorized into different types. Thermoplastic injection molding involves melting the plastic material and injecting it into a mold to form the desired shape. Cold runner molding uses a system where the molten plastic is injected into a cooled runner, leading to the formation of the final product. Hot runner molding follows a similar process, but the runner remains heated for continuous production. Lastly, there are other methods used, which may include gas-assisted molding, insert molding, or stack molding, among others.</p></p>
