<p><p>Urethane Acrylate Resin Market Types can be categorized into low viscosity and high viscosity. Low viscosity urethane acrylate resins have a thinner consistency, making them suitable for applications where easy penetration and flow are required. They are commonly used in coatings and adhesives. On the other hand, high viscosity urethane acrylate resins have a thicker consistency, offering better control and reduced sagging. They are often used in applications where higher build-up and structural support are needed, such as inks and 3D printing.</p></p>
