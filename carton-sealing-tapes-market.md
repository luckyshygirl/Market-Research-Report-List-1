<p><strong>The Carton Sealing Tapes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pressure Sensitive</li><li>Water-activated</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carton sealing tapes are classified into three main types: pressure sensitive, water-activated, and other. Pressure-sensitive tapes are made of adhesive material that instantly bonds to surfaces when pressure is applied. They are easy to use and do not require water or heat for activation. Water-activated tapes, on the other hand, require water to activate the adhesive. These tapes form a strong bond with the carton once they dry. The "other" category includes tapes with unique characteristics or adhesives, such as gummed paper tapes or reinforced tapes, which provide extra strength and security for heavy or fragile packages.</p></p>
