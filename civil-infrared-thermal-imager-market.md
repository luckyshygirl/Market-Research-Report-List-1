<p><p>The civil infrared thermal imager market consists of two main types: uncooled infrared thermal imagers and refrigeration infrared thermal imagers. Uncooled infrared thermal imagers operate without the need for cooling systems, making them more compact and cost-effective. They are mainly used for outdoor applications such as surveillance and firefighting. On the other hand, refrigeration infrared thermal imagers rely on cooling systems to enhance their sensitivity and performance. They are mainly used for industrial processes, medical diagnostics, and scientific research where high image quality is crucial.</p></p>
