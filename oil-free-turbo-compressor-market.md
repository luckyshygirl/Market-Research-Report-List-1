<p><p>The oil-free turbo-compressor market consists of two main types: single stroke turbo compressors and two stroke turbo compressors. Single stroke turbo compressors are designed with a single piston stroke, resulting in a more simplified and compact design. On the other hand, two stroke turbo compressors feature two piston strokes, which allow for increased efficiency and higher compression ratios. Both types eliminate the need for oil lubrication, minimizing maintenance requirements and reducing the risk of oil contamination in the compressed air or gas.</p></p>
