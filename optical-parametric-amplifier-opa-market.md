<p><p>An Optical Parametric Amplifier (OPA) is a device used in laser systems to amplify optical signals. The market for OPA can be classified into two types: tunable wavelength amplifier and fixed wavelength amplifier. A tunable wavelength amplifier allows users to adjust the output wavelength by varying the pump wavelength or wavelength conversion processes. On the other hand, a fixed wavelength amplifier provides amplification only for a specific wavelength. These two types cater to different user requirements in terms of wavelength flexibility and application specificity.</p></p>
