<p><p>In the public space floodlights market, there are two main types: adjustable and fixed floodlights. On the other hand, fixed floodlights have a set position and cannot be adjusted. They provide a constant and stable source of light, ensuring consistent illumination in the public space without the need for manual adjustments.</p></p>
