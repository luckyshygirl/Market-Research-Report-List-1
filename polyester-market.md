<p><p>The polyester market is divided into two main types: saturated polyesters and unsaturated polyesters. Saturated polyesters are commonly used in the production of polyester fibers, films, and engineering plastics. They have high stability, excellent resistance to heat and chemicals, and find application in various industries such as textiles, packaging, and automotive. On the other hand, unsaturated polyesters are widely used in the manufacturing of fiberglass-reinforced plastics and gel coats. These polyesters are generally cured with a catalyst and offer properties like good corrosion resistance and dimensional stability.</p></p>
