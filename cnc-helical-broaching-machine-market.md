<p><p>The CNC Helical Broaching Machine market is segmented into two types: Horizontal Broaching Machine and Vertical Broaching Machine. Horizontal Broaching Machines are designed for horizontal cutting operations, where the workpiece is fixed on a horizontal table and the broach tool moves horizontally to perform the cutting. On the other hand, Vertical Broaching Machines are used for vertical cutting operations, where the workpiece is fixed on a vertical table and the broach tool moves vertically to execute the cutting process. These machines cater to different cutting requirements based on the orientation of the cutting operation.</p></p>
