<p><p>Carbon Fiber-reinforced Plastic (CFRP) is a composite material consisting of carbon fibers embedded in a polymer matrix. CFRP can be broadly categorized into different market types based on the type of precursor material used for the production of carbon fibers. PAN-based CFRP is manufactured using polyacrylonitrile as a precursor material, Pitch-based CFRP is derived from coal tar or petroleum pitch, and Rayon-based CFRP is produced from regenerated cellulose fibers. Other market types may include hybrid CFRP variants or those produced using alternative precursor materials. These categorizations reflect the diverse range of applications and characteristics of CFRP in various industries.</p></p>
