<p><p>The reinforced carbon-carbon (RCC) market can be classified into two types: Chemical Vapor Deposition (CVD) and Liquid Impregnation Process (LIP) markets. In the CVD market, carbon fibers are deposited onto a carbon matrix via a chemical reaction, resulting in a strong and durable material suitable for applications in aerospace and transportation industries. The LIP market, on the other hand, involves impregnating carbon fibers with a liquid resin, enhancing its strength and allowing for the manufacturing of heat-resistant components used in aircraft engines and rocket nozzles. These methods offer versatile solutions for various industrial needs.</p></p>
