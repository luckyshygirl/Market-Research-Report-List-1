<p><p>Air swept hammer mills are grinding mills that are used to pulverize materials, such as grains, minerals, and wood chips, into a fine powder. The air swept hammer mills have an integrated air classifying system, which separates the fine particles from the coarse ones, ensuring a consistent particle size distribution.</p><p>The future outlook of the air swept hammer mills market looks promising.
