<p><p>The automated cash handling device market comprises various types of machines used for processing cash transactions. Cash Validators verify the authenticity of banknotes and coins. Cash Recyclers accept, validate, and dispense cash, serving as both an ATM and a cash dispenser. Rolled Coin Dispensers distribute rolled coins, while Loose Coin Validators authenticate loose coins for deposit or exchange. These devices facilitate convenient and secure handling of cash transactions.</p></p>
