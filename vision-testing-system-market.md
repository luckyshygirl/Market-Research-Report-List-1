<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The vision testing system market comprises various types of tests used to evaluate different aspects of visual function. Full Field Electroretinography (ffERG) measures the electrical responses from the entire retina. Multifocal Electroretinography (mfERG) assesses the functional integrity of various retinal regions. Pattern Electroretinography (ERG) measures the electrical activity of the retina in response to specific visual patterns. Visual Evoked Potential (VEP) measures the electrical activity generated in response to visual stimuli in the brain's visual pathway. Additionally, there are other types of vision testing systems available that cater to specific diagnostic needs in the field of ophthalmology.</p></p>
