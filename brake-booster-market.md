<p><p>The brake booster market comprises three main types: vacuum booster, hydraulic booster, and air pressure booster. Vacuum boosters utilize a vacuum created by the engine to enhance brake power. Hydraulic boosters employ hydraulic pressure from the power steering pump to aid the brake system. Air pressure boosters utilize compressed air to amplify braking force. These boosters enhance the effectiveness of braking systems in vehicles, making them more reliable and efficient, ultimately ensuring safer and smoother braking experiences for drivers.</p></p>
