<p><p>The MEMS accelerometer market in the automotive industry consists of different types of accelerometers. The 3-axis accelerometer measures acceleration along all three axes. The 6-axis accelerometer combines a 3-axis accelerometer with a 3-axis gyroscope, enabling it to measure both linear acceleration and rotation. The 9-axis accelerometer includes a 3-axis accelerometer, 3-axis gyroscope, and 3-axis magnetometer, allowing it to measure linear acceleration, rotation, and magnetic field.</p></p>
