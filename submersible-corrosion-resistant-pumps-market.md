<p><p>The submersible corrosion resistant pumps market consists of two main types - single impeller pumps and dual impeller pumps. Single impeller pumps have only one rotating component that creates pressure to circulate the fluid. On the other hand, dual impeller pumps have two rotating components that enhance the pumping performance by creating higher pressure. Both types of pumps are designed to resist corrosion and can be submerged in liquids without being affected, making them suitable for various applications in industries such as oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing.</p></p>
