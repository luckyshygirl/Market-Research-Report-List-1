These separators utilize a rotating drum in which the magnetic particles are attracted to the magnet and separated from the non-magnetic materials. Dry drum magnetic separators are commonly used in industries such as mining, recycling, and construction.</p><p>2. Wet Magnetic Separators:</p><p>Wet magnetic separators are designed for the separation of magnetic materials from non-magnetic materials in wet conditions. These separators involve the use of a wet slurry mixture, where the magnetic particles are attracted to the magnet while the non-magnetic materials flow freely. Wet magnetic separators are extensively used in industries such as mineral processing, ceramics, and food processing.</p></p>
