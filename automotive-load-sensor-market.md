<p><p>The Automotive Load Sensor market encompasses various types of sensors used in vehicles to measure and monitor load conditions. Wheel Spoke Sensors are installed on the wheel spokes to detect wheel loads and provide information for suspension control. Torque Transducers measure the torque generated by the engine and provide accurate feedback for engine control systems. Hole Sensors are used to measure loads at specific points in the vehicle structure. S-Sensors are sophisticated sensors that provide precise load measurements and are often used in advanced vehicle stability control systems.</p></p>
