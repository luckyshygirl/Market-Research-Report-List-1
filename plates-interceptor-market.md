<p><p>The market for plates interceptors can be divided into three types: Corrugated Plate Interceptor (CPI), Tilted Plate Interceptor (TPI), and Parallel Plate Interceptor (PPI). CPI utilizes corrugated plates to enhance the separation of oil and water, making it effective in oil spill containment. TPI features inclined plates that create a large settling surface, facilitating efficient oil-water separation. PPI employs parallel plates to achieve enhanced separation in high flow conditions. Each interceptor type serves a specific purpose, catering to diverse oil-water separation needs in various industries.</p></p>
