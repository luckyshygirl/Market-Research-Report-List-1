<p><p>The microecological modulator market consists of three main types: probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics. Probiotics are live bacteria that provide health benefits to the host when consumed in adequate amounts. Prebiotics, on the other hand, are substances that stimulate the growth and activity of beneficial bacteria in the digestive system. Synbiotics combine both probiotics and prebiotics, providing a synergistic effect by promoting the growth and survival of beneficial bacteria. These microecological modulators are becoming increasingly popular as they support a healthy gut microbiota and offer various health benefits.</p></p>
