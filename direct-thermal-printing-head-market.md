<p><p>The direct thermal printing head market consists of two main types: thick film printhead and thin film printhead. Thick film printheads are made by printing conductive material onto a substrate using a paste, resulting in a durable and cost-effective solution. On the other hand, thin film printheads are created by depositing a thin layer of conductive material onto a substrate through various techniques like sputtering or chemical vapor deposition. This type offers higher resolution and finer details but at a higher cost. Both options cater to different printing needs and have their own pros and cons.</p></p>
