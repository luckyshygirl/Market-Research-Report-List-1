<p><p>A steel wheel is a circular component typically made of steel, which is used as a medium of contact between a vehicle and the road surface. It forms an essential part of a vehicle's overall structure and plays a crucial role in providing support and stability, as well as facilitating smooth movement. These wheels are predominantly used in heavy commercial vehicles, such as trucks and trailers, due to their robustness and durability.</p><p>The future outlook of the steel wheel market seems promising. The demand for commercial vehicles, particularly in emerging economies, is expected to increase significantly in the coming years. This is attributed to various factors such as expanding e-commerce activities, industrial growth, and improving infrastructure. As a result, there will be a heightened demand for steel wheels to equip these vehicles.</p><p>Additionally, steel wheels are preferred over other materials like aluminum due to their lower cost and better load-bearing capacity. This factor is expected to contribute to the market growth. Moreover, the rising trend of electric commercial vehicles may lead to an increased demand for steel wheels as they provide the necessary strength to support the heavier batteries.</p><p>However, the market may face certain challenges. With the growing emphasis on reducing vehicle weight to improve fuel efficiency, there is a shift towards lightweight materials like aluminum alloy wheels. This shift, coupled with the increasing popularity of tubeless tires, may pose a threat to the steel wheel market.</p><p>In conclusion, the steel wheel market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecasted period.
