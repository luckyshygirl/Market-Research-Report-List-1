<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Organic Light Emitting Diode (OLED) technology is used in various market types. The Hole Transport Layer (HTL) materials help transport positive charges, while Electron Transport Layer (ETL) materials transport negative charges. Electrode materials serve as contacts for the current flow. The Emissive Layer Materials (EML) emit light when charges recombine. Substrates provide structural support. Different market types have varying requirements for these components, such as display panels needing larger HTL and ETL materials, while lighting applications prioritize electrode materials and EML for efficient light emission.</p></p>
