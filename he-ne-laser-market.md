<p><p>A He-Ne (Helium-Neon) laser is a type of gas laser that operates in the visible spectrum. It consists of a mixture of helium and neon gases sealed within a glass tube, with mirrors at each end to form an optical resonator. When an electric current is applied to the gas mixture, it excites the helium and neon atoms, resulting in the emission of laser light with a characteristic wavelength of 632.8 nm, which corresponds to red light.</p><p>The He-Ne laser market has witnessed significant growth in recent years and is expected to continue its upward trajectory in the forecasted period. Factors driving market growth include the increasing demand for laser technology in various applications such as spectroscopy, confocal microscopy, holography, barcode scanners, laser printers, and alignment systems.</p><p>The advent of new laser applications, such as laser-based displays and automotive LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) systems, is expected to provide additional growth opportunities for the He-Ne laser market. Moreover, ongoing advancements in laser technology, such as the development of high-power He-Ne lasers and miniaturized laser systems, are anticipated to further fuel market growth.</p><p>Additionally, the growing demand for precision measurement tools, scientific research equipment, and medical devices that utilize He-Ne lasers is expected to contribute to market expansion.
