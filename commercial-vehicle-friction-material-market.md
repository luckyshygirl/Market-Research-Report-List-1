<p><p>The commercial vehicle friction material market is segmented into three main types: brake linings, brake pads, and clutch facings. </p><p>Brake linings are a type of friction material used in commercial vehicle braking systems to provide friction and convert kinetic energy into heat, ultimately stopping the vehicle. </p><p>Brake pads are another type of friction material that also play a key role in vehicle braking. Like brake linings, brake pads are designed to generate friction and heat to stop the vehicle effectively. </p><p>Lastly, clutch facings are friction materials used in the clutch system of commercial vehicles, enabling smooth engagement and disengagement of the transmission to control the vehicle's speed and power.</p></p>
