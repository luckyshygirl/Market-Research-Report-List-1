<p><ul><li>Solvay Method</li><li>Trona Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy soda ash market consists of two main types: the Solvay method and the Trona method. In the Solvay method, soda ash is produced by combining salt, lime, ammonia, and carbon dioxide. This process is energy-intensive but yields high purity soda ash suitable for various industrial applications. On the other hand, the Trona method involves extracting soda ash from naturally occurring trona ore through a series of chemical reactions. This method is considered more environmentally friendly and cost-effective, making it increasingly popular in the heavy soda ash market.</p></p>
