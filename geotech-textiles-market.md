<p><p>The geotech textiles market consists of various types of fabrics used in geotechnical applications. Woven fabric refers to textiles made by interlacing warp and weft threads, offering high tensile strength and stability. Non-woven fabric is made by bonding fibers together without weaving, providing excellent filtration and drainage properties. Knitted fabric is produced by interlocking loops of yarn, providing flexibility and breathability. Other types include membranes, grids, and composites, offering specialized functionalities in geotechnical projects. All these fabric types serve different purposes, contributing to the wide range of applications in the geotech textiles market.</p></p>
