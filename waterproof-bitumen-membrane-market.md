<p><p>The waterproof bitumen membrane market is categorized into SBS type and APP type. SBS (styrene butadiene styrene) type membranes are modified with elastomers, providing flexibility and enhancing durability. These membranes have excellent resistance to high temperature variations and are commonly used in areas with extreme weather conditions. On the other hand, APP (atactic polypropylene) type membranes are modified with polypropylene, making them more resistant to UV radiation and heat. These membranes are ideal for regions with prolonged exposure to sunlight. Overall, both types provide reliable waterproofing solutions for various construction projects.</p></p>
