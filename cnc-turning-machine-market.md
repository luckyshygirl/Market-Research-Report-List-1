<p><p>The CNC turning machine market can be divided into two main types based on spindle orientation: horizontal and vertical. In horizontal CNC turning machines, the spindle is positioned horizontally, allowing for easy loading and unloading of the workpiece. This type of machine is suitable for large-sized or long workpieces. On the other hand, vertical CNC turning machines have a vertically oriented spindle, making them more suitable for machining small or medium-sized workpieces. The choice of spindle orientation depends on the specific requirements and size of the workpiece to be machined.</p></p>
