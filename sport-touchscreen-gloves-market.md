Resistive Touchscreen Gloves are designed with a special layer that enables touch capabilities by applying pressure on the screen. They are generally less sensitive and offer higher durability. On the other hand, Capacitive Touchscreen Gloves use conductive materials to create a connection with the screen, providing better responsiveness and accuracy. These gloves are more commonly used due to their ability to offer precise control while engaging in sporting activities.</p></p>
