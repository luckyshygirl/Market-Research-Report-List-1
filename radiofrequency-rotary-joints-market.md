<p><p>The Radiofrequency Rotary Joints market includes two types: Single and Multi. A Single Rotary Joint allows the transmission of RF signals between two stationary elements, providing a reliable connection. On the other hand, a Multi Rotary Joint enables the transmission of RF signals between one stationary element and multiple rotating elements. This type is suitable for applications where multiple connections are required simultaneous with rotation. Both types are crucial in various industries where uninterrupted and reliable RF signal transmission is essential.</p></p>
