<p><p>The automotive over-the-air (OTA) update market includes two main types: Firmware over-the-air (FOTA) and Software over-the-air (SOTA). FOTA focuses on updating and improving the vehicle's operating system and electronic control units, enabling new features and fixing bugs. On the other hand, SOTA emphasizes updating applications and software components to enhance functionality, user experience, and security. Both FOTA and SOTA are essential for automakers to ensure their vehicles stay up to date with the latest technology advancements and offer a seamless user experience.</p></p>
