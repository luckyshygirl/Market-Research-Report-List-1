<p><p>Endoscopic suction-irrigation sets are instruments used during endoscopic procedures to provide suction and irrigation capabilities. They are available in different market types: disposable, semi-disposable, and reusable. Disposable sets are designed for single patient use and are discarded after each procedure, ensuring hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination. Semi-disposable sets can be used multiple times by reprocessing some components while discarding others. Reusable sets are designed to be sterilized and reused multiple times, offering cost savings but requiring thorough cleaning and sterilization processes to maintain patient safety.</p></p>
