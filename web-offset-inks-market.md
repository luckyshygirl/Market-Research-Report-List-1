<p><p>Web offset inks refer to the type of inks used in web offset printing, a method commonly used for high-speed commercial printing. There are two main types of web offset inks: coldset inks and heatset inks. Coldset inks are used for printing on absorbent materials like newsprint, where the ink dries by absorbing into the paper. Heatset inks, on the other hand, are used for printing on coated or glossy papers, where the ink is dried using heat or infrared lamps. Both types of inks have their specific applications and requirements based on the printing substrate.</p></p>
