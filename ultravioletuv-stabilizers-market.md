<p><strong>The Ultraviolet(UV) Stabilizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hindered Amine Light Stabilizer(HALS)</li><li>UV Absorbers</li><li>Quencher</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultraviolet (UV) Stabilizers market consists of several types. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are one such type, which provide excellent long-term protection against UV light by trapping and neutralizing free radicals. UV Absorbers, on the other hand, prevent UV radiation from reaching the material by absorbing and converting it into heat. Quenchers are substances that help deactivate excited states of molecules, preventing them from undergoing reactions that could lead to degradation. Lastly, the "Other" market category includes additional types of UV stabilizers with unique functionalities not covered by the aforementioned categories.</p></p>
