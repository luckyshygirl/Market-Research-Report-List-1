<p><p>The bilirubin meter market includes two types: bench-top and transcutaneous. Bench-top bilirubin meters are stand-alone instruments used in laboratory settings for measuring bilirubin levels in blood samples. They provide accurate results and are frequently used by healthcare professionals. On the other hand, transcutaneous bilirubin meters are portable devices used on the skin surface to measure bilirubin levels. They offer non-invasive testing and are often utilized in neonatal care, allowing for quick and convenient monitoring of bilirubin levels in newborns without the need for blood samples.</p></p>
