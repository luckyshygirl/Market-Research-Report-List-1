<p><p>The automatic torque converter market can be classified into two types: single-stage torque converter and multistage torque converter. In a single-stage torque converter, there is only one set of turbines and stator blades, resulting in a simpler and more cost-effective design. On the other hand, a multistage torque converter consists of multiple sets of turbines and stator blades, offering improved torque multiplication and efficiency. These different types cater to varying needs and preferences in terms of performance, cost, and efficiency in the automatic torque converter market.</p></p>
