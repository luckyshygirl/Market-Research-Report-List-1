<p><p>The water absorbing crystals market can be categorized into two types: powder and free-flowing granular. Powder water absorbing crystals refer to fine particles that have a high capacity to absorb and retain water. They are commonly used in applications where quick absorption is required due to their higher surface area. On the other hand, free-flowing granular water absorbing crystals are larger particles that allow for better flow and movement. They are typically used in applications where controlled release of water is desired, such as in agriculture or gardening.</p></p>
