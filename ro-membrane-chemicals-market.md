<p><p>The RO Membrane Chemicals Market is categorized into three main types: antiscalants, cleaning chemicals, and pretreatment chemicals. Antiscalants are used to prevent the build-up of scale on the membrane surface, which can reduce performance. Cleaning chemicals are employed to remove fouling and restore the membrane's efficiency. Pretreatment chemicals are used to condition feedwater before it reaches the membrane, preventing issues such as oxidation and microbial growth. Each type of chemical serves a different purpose in maintaining and ensuring the optimal performance of reverse osmosis membranes.</p></p>
