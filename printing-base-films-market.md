<p><ul><li>Biaxially Stretched Film</li><li>Unidirectional Stretched Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printing base films market includes two main types: biaxially stretched film and unidirectional stretched film. Biaxially stretched film is produced by stretching the film in both the machine direction and the transverse direction, resulting in improved tensile strength, clarity, and dimensional stability. On the other hand, unidirectional stretched film is produced by stretching the film in only one direction, providing enhanced strength and elongation properties. Both types of films are widely used in printing applications for packaging, labels, and other purposes to ensure high-quality print results.</p></p>
