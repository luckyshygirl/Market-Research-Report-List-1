<p><p>The Aircraft Door Damper market consists of various types of dampers. Rotary Aircraft Dampers work by using a rotary motion to control the movement of aircraft doors, providing smooth and controlled opening and closing. Linear Aircraft Dampers use a linear motion mechanism to achieve the same purpose. Axial Aircraft Dampers, on the other hand, utilize an axial motion to dampen vibrations and prevent in-flight noise. These different types of dampers cater to the specific needs and requirements of aircraft doors.</p></p>
