<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The SUV & Pickup Wheel Speed Sensor market includes two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall effect sensor to determine wheel speed by measuring the magnetic field generated by a rotating toothed wheel or gear. These sensors are more accurate and have higher resolution. On the other hand, Magnetic Electric Type sensors utilize a magnet and an electric coil to detect the wheel's rotational speed. They are simpler in design and less expensive. Both types play a vital role in providing precise wheel speed information in SUVs and pickup trucks.</p></p>
