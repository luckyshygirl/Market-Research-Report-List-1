<p><p>The lithography filter market can be classified into different types, including Nylon66 membrane and HDPE membrane. Nylon66 membranes are a type of filter material made from polyamide 66, which offers excellent chemical resistance and high temperature stability. These membranes are commonly used in lithography filters due to their superior filtration efficiency and durability. On the other hand, HDPE membranes are made from high-density polyethylene and are known for their excellent resistance to chemicals and abrasion. They are also widely used in lithography filters for their high flow rates and outstanding particle retention capabilities.</p></p>
