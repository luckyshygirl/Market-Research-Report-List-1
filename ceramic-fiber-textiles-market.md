<p><p>Ceramic fiber textiles, including cloth, tape, twisted rope, and yarn, are products made from ceramic fibers that possess excellent thermal insulation properties. Cloth refers to a flexible sheet-like material used for insulation purposes, while tape is a narrow strip used for wrapping or sealing applications. Twisted rope is constructed by twisting ceramic fibers together, offering a more robust form. Finally, yarn is a strand of ceramic fibers used for weaving or knitting applications. Each type of ceramic fiber textile serves as a versatile solution for various industries requiring high-temperature insulation and protection.</p></p>
