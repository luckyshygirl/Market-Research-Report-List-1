<p><p>The Horseshoe Lifebuoy market consists of two main types: Inherent Type and Inflatable Type. Inherent Type lifebuoys are made of solid material and do not require inflation. They are ready to use in emergency situations and offer immediate buoyancy. In contrast, Inflatable Type lifebuoys need to be manually inflated or can inflate automatically upon contact with water. They are compact and easy to store but might take more time to deploy. Both types cater to different needs and preferences, ensuring the safety of individuals in and around water bodies.</p></p>
