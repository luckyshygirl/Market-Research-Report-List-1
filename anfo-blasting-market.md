<p><p>ANFO blasting refers to the application of Ammonium Nitrate Fuel Oil (ANFO) explosives in various industries. The market is categorized into three types: powdered ammonium oil explosive, porous granular ammonium oil explosive, and heavy ammonium oil explosive. Powdered ANFO explosives are finely-ground mixtures used for general blasting purposes. Porous granular ANFO explosives are larger particles with increased porosity, suitable for underground mining. Heavy ANFO explosives consist of denser particles, designed for specialized applications such as seismic exploration. These different market types cater to specific needs and requirements within the blasting industry.</p></p>
