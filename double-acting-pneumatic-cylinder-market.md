<p><p>Double-Acting Pneumatic Cylinder market comprises various types, including Rotary Pneumatic Cylinder and Semi-Rotary Pneumatic Cylinder. Rotary Pneumatic Cylinder operates in a rotational motion, making it suitable for applications where limited linear motion is required, such as turning objects or valves. On the other hand, Semi-Rotary Pneumatic Cylinder combines both rotary and linear motion, making it effective for applications that require both radial and linear movement, such as indexing or positioning. Both types of cylinders play a crucial role in diverse industries, providing efficient and reliable pneumatic power for various tasks.</p></p>
