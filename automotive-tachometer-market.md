<p><p>The automotive tachometer market can be classified into two types: analog tachometers and digital tachometers. Analog tachometers display the engine's RPM (revolutions per minute) using a dial and a needle. They are traditionally used in older vehicles and offer a simple and straightforward display. On the other hand, digital tachometers provide a numerical display of the RPM on a digital screen. They are commonly found in newer vehicles and offer a more precise and accurate reading. Both types serve the purpose of measuring the engine's RPM but differ in their display mechanism.</p></p>
