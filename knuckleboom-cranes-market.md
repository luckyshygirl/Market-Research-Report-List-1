<p><p>Knuckleboom cranes are versatile lifting machines fitted with a pivoting boom and a joint near the middle for increased flexibility. The market is categorized into three types based on boom length: small, medium, and long boom cranes. Small boom cranes have shorter reach and are ideal for compact spaces or lighter loads. Medium boom cranes offer a balance between reach and load capacity, serving various industries. Long boom cranes have extended reach capabilities, making them useful for construction, forestry, or marine applications where lifting heavy loads over long distances is required.</p></p>
