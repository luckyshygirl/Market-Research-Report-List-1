<p><p>The high-strength epoxy adhesives market is categorized into three main types: one-component, two-component, and others. One-component adhesives are pre-catalyzed, ready-to-use adhesives that do not require mixing with any other component before application. Two-component adhesives consist of two separate components, resin, and hardener, which need to be mixed together before use. This type provides a longer working time before curing. The "others" category includes specialty epoxy adhesives that possess unique properties or are designed for specific applications, such as underwater adhesives or adhesives with enhanced flexibility.</p></p>
